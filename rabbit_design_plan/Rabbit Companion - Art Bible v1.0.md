# Rabbit Companion - Art Bible v1.0

## Core Concept

A cozy pixel-art companion app.

The companion starts as a baby rabbit and gradually grows into a rabbit girl and eventually an elegant woman.

She is not a waifu or a collectible.

She is a lifelong companion whose appearance, personality and lifestyle reflect the user's habits.

Core style:

- Cute pixel art
- Anime chibi
- Cozy atmosphere
- Warm pastel colors
- Transparent background
- Consistent blue eyes
- White rabbit ears throughout all stages
- Same personality across all forms
- 64x64 sprites
- 4~8 frame animations
